My Story

I'm here because...

I want to learn design things based on how I want them to look!

Right after college, I traveled for 6 months and tried my best to capture my stories and highlights through Wordpress. I was frustrated by my lack of design skills and freedom to customize pre-designed template. That's when I became interested in web development.

Then I landed a job in Software. After a few roles, I deliver and create technical training. I aspire to speak the same language as developers so I can train them. I'd also like to use these development skills to design a travel website like I've always wanted to!
